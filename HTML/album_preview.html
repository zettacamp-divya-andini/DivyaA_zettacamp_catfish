Butter
known digitally as Butter / Permission to Dance[1][2]) is the first English single album by BTS. It was released on July 9, 2021. It was certified Platinum for selling more than 250,000 copies by the RIAJ in July 2021.

What does BTS butter album contain?
1 CD, 1 Limited Edition Poster (2 versions), 1 Photo Book (2 versions), 2 Lyric Cards, 1 Photo Card (1 of 8 each random), 1 Instant Photo Card, 1 Graphic Sticker (2 versions), 1 Folded Message Card (1 of 7 each random), 1 Photo Stand (2 versions). All sales count towards the Korean Hanteo and Gaon charts.
